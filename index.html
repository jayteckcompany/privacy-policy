**JayTeck Universal Privacy Policy** *Last updated: July 2025* This privacy policy applies to all software, browser extensions, mobile applications, websites, and digital tools developed and published by **JayTeck** ("we," "us," or "our"). Scope This policy covers all digital products created by JayTeck, including: * Chrome Extensions * Mobile Applications (iOS/Android) * Web Tools or Hosted Apps * Other downloadable or embedded software Unless explicitly stated otherwise in a product-specific notice, this general privacy policy applies. --- ### 2. Data We Do **Not** Collect By default, we do **not**: * Collect personally identifiable information (PII) * Track user activity or browsing history * Transmit or store any data off the user’s device * Use cookies or trackers (unless otherwise disclosed in a specific app) * Sell, rent, or share user data with third parties --- ### 3. Chrome Extensions Our browser extensions: * Do **not** collect, transmit, or store personal or sensitive user data * Only run in response to **user action** (e.g., clicking a button) * Access specific pages (e.g., `qbo.intuit.com`) solely for the purpose of reading data visible on-screen * Operate entirely within the browser and do **not** send data to remote servers Permissions requested (like `activeTab`, `scripting`, and `host_permissions`) are strictly used to fulfill the intended feature, such as reading values on a page to calculate totals.
